Our Story

Welcome to Golden Spice Restaurant, a culinary gem nestled in the heart of Dhanmondi, Dhaka. Since 2010, we have been serving our guests with the finest traditional and contemporary dishes prepared with love and the freshest ingredients.

Our expert chefs bring decades of experience, combining age-old recipes with modern culinary techniques to create dishes that delight your taste buds and warm your soul.
